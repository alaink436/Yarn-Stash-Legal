YarnStash

The all-in-one app for knitters and crocheters — organize your yarn, track your projects, find patterns that fit your stash.

What it does

  • Stash management — log every yarn with brand, weight, fiber, color and yardage; sort by colour or weight automatically.
  • Smart matching — find Ravelry patterns that fit your existing yarn, or yarns that fit a pattern you're eyeing.
  • Label scanning — point the camera at a yarn label, the app extracts brand, weight, fiber and colour automatically.
  • Project tracking — row counter, progress bar, photo log, status (planned/in progress/finished/frogged).
  • Knit-Group sharing — share your stash with up to 3 family members or friends.
  • Wishlist & shopping list — keep track of yarns and patterns you want to buy.

Privacy

  • All data stored on EU servers (Frankfurt) with end-to-end encryption in transit and at rest.
  • No ads. No data sales. GDPR-compliant.
  • Account deletion removes all your data within 30 days.
